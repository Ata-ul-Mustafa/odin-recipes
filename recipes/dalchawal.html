Dal Chawal

Description

It consists of steamed rice and a cooked lentil or other pulses stew called dal. It is a staple food in south asian countries.Chawal means "boiled rice" in a number of Indo-Aryan languages.

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chillies, chopped
  • 1 t-spoon minced ginger
  • salt to taste
  • 1/2 t-spoon turmeric powdered
  • 1 cup Rice
  • a pinch of salt
  • 1 cup Split Red Masoor dal
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 t-spoon garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 table-spoon oil
  • 3 cup water
  • 1/2 t-spoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 t-spoon Mustard seeds
  • For Chawal (Rice) : 1 cup Rice (Normal or Basmati) 2 cups water

Steps

For Dal

  1. Wash Masoor dal 3-4 times with water and soak for 15 minutes.
  2. Pressure cook dal with 3 cup water,turmeric and pinch of salt over medium-high heat for about 15 mins. or until it gets tender.
  3. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  4. Splutter cumin seeds, Mustard and chopped green chillies, stir it for 15-30 secs.
  5. Then add onion, garlic, ginger, and cook for about 3-4 mins. stirring frequently.
  6. Now, add cooked Masoor dal mixture and Coriander powder to the large frying pan and turn the heat to low, stir it well, cover the pan and cook further for 10 mins.
  7. or until the dal gets soften and gets little dissolved in a mixture.

For Chawal

  1. Wash rice 3-4 times with water.
  2. Combine it with water and a pinch of salt in a pressure cooker and cook for about 8-10 mins.
  3. Over medium-high flame.
  4. Serve rice in a plate and pour hot dal on it.
